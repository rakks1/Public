What We Offer ?

At Magic Bus Helping Hands Foundation, we provide:

  • Accessibility Resources:Tools and guidance for independent living.
    Information on assistive technologies to enhance independence.
  • Advocacy: Support for rights, inclusion, and reducing stigma.
    Awareness programs on the rights of persons with disabilities.
  • Skills & Employment: Training and job opportunities tailored to abilities.
    Partnerships with organizations to create employment opportunities.
  • Community Engagement: Events, peer support, and online forums.
    Events and meetups to foster a sense of belonging and collaboration.
  • Caregiver Support: Resources and training for better care.
    Networking opportunities with other caregivers to share insights.
  • Educational Assistance: Inclusive education support and scholarships.
    Awareness sessions for educators and institutions.

We are here to empower and uplift individuals with disabilities.

Together, we can build a more inclusive world for all.
